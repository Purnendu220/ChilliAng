About Chili

MTML launched ‘CHiLi’ GSM services in Mauritius, in November 2011. Using the latest technology, CHiLi offers greater choice for Voice, Data (3G & LTE ) at affordable prices to the customers.

MTML has heavily invested on CHiLi network and the equipment are supplied by leading Telecom equipment manufacturers around the world. The advanced technology is very environment friendly and allows reduction in electricity consumption of approx. 60% compared to traditional equipment.

CHiLi- brand & service is designed around the needs of the customer. The philosophy is to provide Technology that empowers the individual, at affordable costs to the People, and delivered in a way which is fun and caring to suit the Lifestyle of the customers. The CHiLi motto…”The coolest you’ll get”.

CHiLi offers the most attractive Tariff in the market, on an average around 60% economical than the competitors with the added advantage of also having lower International Call rates. CHiLi is the first & only network providing Incoming Call Bonus, where the customer get paid for receiving calls from other networks.
